  About Us

Welcome to the Student Placement Website of IIT Jodhpur!

Founded in 2008, IIT Jodhpur has emerged in developing a distinct culture of engineering and science education and other areas of scholarship, to best fulfill the current and emerging needs of the changing Indian Society. IIT Jodhpur close association with leading industries and research institutes effectively helps student gain hands on experience and solve technical challenges. As on date, the Institute has 848 students spread across 555 B.Tech. students, 56 M.Tech. students, 71 M.Sc. students and 166 Ph.D. students pursuing various academic programs Also, this year we have upcoming Department of Metallurgical and Material Engineering, which offers courses in M.Tech. and Ph.D. The department teaching, research and consulting activities will cover a broad spectrum of material science and industrial metallurgy. IIT Jodhpur is also making research in addressing national needs especially those leading to the development of technology for diagnostic and treatment by medical professionals with its new Department of Biological Science and Bioengineering which is planned towards Biological science, medical science, technology and their interfaces. The department will offer a wide range of courses from foundation level to advanced level in the said domains for the various degree programs. Hands-on learning will be emphasized using state-of-the-art centralized research facilities.

IIT Jodhpur has moved to its sprawling 852 acre residential Permanent Campus on National Highway 65 (NNE of Jodhpur towards Nagaur). The Permanent Campus is meticulously planned and envisioned to stand as a symbol of academics - simple, but deep.

IIT Jodhpur promotes technology thought and action. It is on the mission to prepare competent technology graduates. We, the office of student placement aims to create an encouraging atmosphere for students by providing them ample reminders to build competencies in sync with their dream career , thereby ensuring their smooth landing in the professional world. Also, it will sensitize students in best possible way by promoting them to take responsibility for their own career decision and prepare them for the industry. Thus, it will provide services and resources that assist the students in their ongoing efforts of competence developing, career planning and employer searching. Office of student placement had a dedicated workforce of students and volunteer students, who are equipped to help guests visiting from the Employer organization acquaint themselves with the placement procedure and provide necessary logistic equipment.

We extend a cordial invitation to you to participate in Campus Placement Drive for the season 2018-19 and hope that you will find the best talent you were looking for.
